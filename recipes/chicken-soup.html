Chicken Soup Recipe

Description

Ingredients:


  • 3-4 pieces of chicken bones
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • necessary amount of salt
  • 1 carrot
  • parsley
  • 1 potatoe
  • 200 gr rice (optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil the chicken bones and the chopped onion in a pressure cooker for 1 hour
  2. Add the broth into a stewpot
  3. Add the rice and olive oil
  4. Add the spices
  5. Thinly slice the carrot and potatoe, add it to the pot
  6. Let it cook for 20 minutes
  7. Serve it with parsley, enjoy!
