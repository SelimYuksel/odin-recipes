Kebap Recipe

Description

Ingredients:


  • 1 tablespoon tomato sauce
  • 1 onion juice
  • 1 tablespoon of yoghurt
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper, salt
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • 500 gr meat
  • 1 sweet onion
  • chopped parsley

Steps:

  1. Add the tomato sauce, the onion juice, the oil and the yoghurt into a bowl
  2. Add the spices and mix them well
  3. Add the thinly sliced meat into the mix
  4. Let it sit for a day
  5. Pour the mix into a heated pan
  6. Cook it evenly on medium heat
  7. Add the cooked dish into a plate and make it look good with sweet onions and parsley :D
